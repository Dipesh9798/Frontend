Introduction

From modern warfare to weather bots, new delivery services to cool video games, it appears that remote controlled aerial-vehicles are everywhere. Ever wondered what will it be like to control one for real? Or even better, build one for yourself? Turn your wishful thinking into reality this Prayukti. An all new event is being introduced in Prayukti 2k19 - Udaan - a competitive team event for aviation enthusiasts. Build a remote-controlled plane from scratch, maneuver it expertly and don't crash - that's all you need to do to come out on top in Udaan. A test of your engineering skills as well as your hand-eye coordination, Udaan is ready to make your dreams fly.
